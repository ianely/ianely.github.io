Found It!

Geocaching is a global treasure hunt that involves finding hidden containers around the world. Found It! delves into this strange hobby, explains what it is, and explores the personal stories of some of the people who play it.

Love Thy Neighbor

The signs and stuffed animals hanging from nooses in front of James Edward Avery’s house on Mason Mill Rd. may seem offensive and even disturbing, but nobody has really tried to understand the point that he’s trying to make. Love Thy Neighbor will uncover the story of James Edward Avery and his signs, as well as his neighbor’s opinions of him and the current legal battle that could put him and his signs behind bars.
